# F6S.com — startup community + database

**URL:** https://www.f6s.com/
**Aanmelding:** Maak een account → "Add a Startup" of "Add my company"

## Hoe

1. Sign up als founder (gebruik je BotLease-email)
2. Klik "Add Startup"
3. Vul de fields zoals in `04-techleap.md` (zelfde info)
4. **Belangrijk:** vul de "Website" velden volledig in — F6S gebruikt deze voor de profiel-URL en backlink
5. Voeg een 3-paragraaf company description, founders, en team toe
6. Upload een vierkant logo (download `/frontend/img/hero/hero-robot.webp` en crop tot 1:1)

## Description (copy-paste)

**Short pitch (1 paragraph):**
> BotLease is the first Dutch operational-lease company for humanoid robots. We make next-generation robotics accessible to Dutch SMEs by handling the full lifecycle: acquisition, EU AI-Act compliance, installation, training, 24h swap-SLA, and insurance — for a fixed monthly fee from €290.

**Long description (3 paragraphs):**
> The humanoid robotics market is exploding. Goldman Sachs projects $38B by 2035, and 2025 was the first year humanoids hit mass production at scale (NEURA Robotics in Germany with Bosch, UBTECH Walker S2 in China with BYD/Foxconn, Apptronik Apollo pilots at Mercedes-Benz, Figure 02 at BMW Spartanburg). For European SMEs, however, the gap between "the technology exists" and "I can deploy it next quarter" is enormous: capex of €20k-€100k+ per unit, EU AI-Act compliance under Regulation 2023/1230, swap-logistics, insurance, training, and integration.
>
> BotLease closes that gap. We carry a curated catalog of 15 humanoid models — from compact EU-built platforms (NEURA 4NE-1 Mini, Pollen Reachy 2) to industrial flagships (NEURA 4NE-1 Gen 3.5, PAL Kangaroo, UBTECH Walker S2). We don't represent any one manufacturer exclusively. We compare independently, advise based on use-case, and operate the asset on behalf of the customer for the full 36-month lease term.
>
> Founded in Amsterdam in 2026 by Thomas Vedder, BotLease focuses on the Dutch and Benelux market. Our differentiators: native Dutch service stack, 24h swap-SLA, EU AI-Act paperwork done for the client, and an independent comparison framework. The product is live at botlease.nl.
