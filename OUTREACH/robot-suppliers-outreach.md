# Robot-leveranciers outreach — verzendklaar

**Hoe te gebruiken:** verstuur vanuit je eigen mailbox (hallo@botlease.nl of persoonlijk) zodat
antwoorden bij jou binnenkomen. Gebruik de master-mail hieronder en vervang per regel uit de tabel
**[BEDRIJF]** en de **modellen-zin**. Bij "FORMULIER" plak je de body in hun contactformulier
(zet "Partnership / Sales inquiry" in het onderwerpveld).

## Master-mail

**Subject:** `Partnership inquiry: humanoid robot supply for BotLease (Netherlands)`

Hi **[BEDRIJF]** team,

My name is Thomas Vedder, founder of BotLease (https://botlease.nl), a Netherlands-based company that leases humanoid robots to Dutch and European businesses.

I'll be honest about where we are. BotLease is young, but it's taken off faster than we expected, and we're now getting genuine customer inquiries and lease requests. The leasing and financing side is well covered: we have people on the team with real experience in leasing, finance, and the car-leasing industry. The one piece we don't yet have in place is direct access to robots, and that's exactly where I'm hoping we can work together.

Would **[BEDRIJF]** be open to supplying **[MODELLEN-ZIN]** to us for lease and deployment in the Netherlands and the wider EU? A few things to get a conversation started:

- Do you work with resellers or lease partners, or do you prefer to supply direct, and on what terms?
- Availability and lead times into the EU
- Indicative pricing, and any partner or dealer pricing at volume
- EU compliance: CE marking, Machinery Regulation (EU) 2023/1230, and AI-Act readiness

Most of all, do you have ideas on how we could help each other here? I'd welcome a short call to explore it.

You can see what we're building at https://botlease.nl.

Warm regards,
Thomas Vedder
Founder, BotLease
https://botlease.nl · hallo@botlease.nl
