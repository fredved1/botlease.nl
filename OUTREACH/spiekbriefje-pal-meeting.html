"Is er echt vraag?"

"Yes. A humanoid taskforce of a large Belgian industrial group contacted us, and one of Europe's largest hardware rental companies wants to explore humanoid rental with us. The demand is real - I am now building the supply side properly."

5 · De lege-stoel-pitch (je sterkste zin)

"As far as I can see, PAL has no official partner in the Netherlands, while you already have customers here - like the StockBots at Decathlon. That seat seems to be empty. I would like to earn it. Properly this time."
  • Feit: geen enkele NL-concurrent voert PAL (Smartrobot = Unitree/UBTECH, robotverhuur.nl = NAO/Pepper). Jij was de enige — daarom vielen ze over jou, én daarom is de stoel echt leeg.
  • Extra haakje: "Who handles local support for the Decathlon StockBots here? Is there room for a Dutch partner doing demos, first-line service and lead generation?"
  • Onderzoeksklanten-haakje: "Universities often need a TIAGo for one project, not forever. We rent it monthly - you sell more units without doing the logistics."
