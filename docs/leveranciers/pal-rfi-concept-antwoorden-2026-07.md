### 2. Knowledge and experience description
BotLease is a young, specialised Dutch company focused on one thing: making humanoid robots practically deployable for organisations in the Netherlands and Belgium through rental (events, pilots) and operational lease. We combine three things that are rare in one place in the Dutch market today: (1) dedicated commercial focus on humanoid robotics, (2) a strong digital presence (botlease.nl ranks #1 on Google.nl for the main Dutch humanoid leasing search terms and generates inbound B2B demand, including requests from large Dutch enterprises), and (3) a deployment model with operator included, so customers get a working demonstration or pilot instead of a box. The founder has a background in AI and automation; compliance (EU Machinery Regulation, AI Act, GDPR) is treated as a core competence rather than an afterthought.
